Features

  • Emergency reach out:
    Case of an emergency, the app allows users to quickly and easily reach out to emergency services such as the police, fire department, and ambulance.
  • Crime finder:
    The app has a feature that helps users find crime hotspots and high-risk areas in their vicinity so that they can take necessary precautions.
  • Rules to be followed in a crime scene:
    The app provides guidelines on how to behave and what actions to take in a crime scene to avoid tampering with evidence or putting oneself in danger.
  • Real-time crime updates:
    The app keeps users informed about crime incidents and alerts them to any potential danger in their area in real-time.
  • Anonymous reporting:
    The app allows users to report crimes anonymously, ensuring their safety and privacy while still helping to keep their community safe.
